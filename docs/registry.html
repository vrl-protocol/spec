About the Registry

Open & Immutable

The circuit registry is an append-only catalog. Once a circuit is registered, it is immutable. New versions can be submitted, but old versions remain available.

Three Tiers

EXPERIMENTAL circuits are newly submitted. REVIEWED circuits have been checked by VRL maintainers. CERTIFIED circuits have been independently audited.

Domains

Circuits are organized by domain: Trade (tariffs, logistics), Healthcare (clinical decisions), Finance (credit, fraud), and General (infrastructure).

Certification Tiers

Certified

CERTIFIED

Independently audited, formally verified, and approved for high-stakes use. Suitable for regulated industries (healthcare, finance, trade).

Reviewed

REVIEWED

Reviewed by VRL core maintainers. Considered production-ready but not independently audited. Good for most enterprise use.

Experimental

EXPERIMENTAL

Newly submitted, not yet reviewed. Use for testing and development only. Subject to change.

Domains

Trade

Circuits for international trade, tariff calculations, export compliance, and customs verification. Used by supply chain and trade finance.

Healthcare

Circuits for clinical decision support, drug interactions, treatment recommendations, and FDA SaMD compliance. Used by hospitals and health systems.

Finance

Circuits for credit risk, fraud detection, algorithmic trading, and regulatory compliance. Used by banks and fintech.

General

Infrastructure circuits: TEE attestation verification, API hash binding, and proof graph composition. Used across all domains.
